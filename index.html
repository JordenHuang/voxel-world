Minecraft-like voxel game using TypeScript and WebGL.
The left one is the original one. The right one uses custom ECS architecture, though I should use an existing engine, as mine is quite bad.

  • Click to enter full screen, and ESC to quit restore
  • w/a/s/d: Move forward/left/backward/right
  • r: Reset
  • Space: Move up
  • Left Shift: Move down
  • Left Control: Speed up (can jump multiple times in the ECS one)
